The Plan

My ultimate objective for myself is to be able to sustain myself solely via the creation of art, and I'm so close that I can almost taste it. And I want to encourage myself to never give up and to never be scared to take a risk in order to reach my objective. I want to meet as many people as possible and share my talents. And I imagine myself surrounded by art in my huge and pleasant house and workshop in ten years.
